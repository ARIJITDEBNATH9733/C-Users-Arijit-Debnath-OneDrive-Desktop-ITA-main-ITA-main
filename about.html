History

Itahar Govt Polytechnic was established in 2019 at Sohair, Itahar in the district of Uttar Dinajpur. This college was set up with the aim of increasing the number of skilled technical professionals in departments not available in the nearby regions. It is approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and affiliated with West Bengal State Council of Technical & Vocational Education & Skill Development (WBSCT&VE&SD) with three Diploma Engineering courses: 1. Computer Science and Technology 2. Electrical Engineering 3. Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.

ACADEMIC OVERVIEW
